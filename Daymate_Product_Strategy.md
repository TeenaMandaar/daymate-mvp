# Daymate: Product Strategy & MVP Overview

## 1. Core Idea
Daymate is India’s first curated hangout platform designed for individuals who hate going solo to public activities. Unlike dating apps which carry romantic pressure, or meetup groups which can be intimidating, Daymate facilitates strictly 1-on-1, platonic hangouts. Users select a real-world activity (like grabbing coffee, watching a movie, or attending a comedy show) and pay a fixed base fee. Daymate then matches them with a manually verified, background-checked companion who is ready to join them for that specific activity.

## 2. Brand Positioning
Daymate is positioned as a **safe, premium, and strictly platonic** lifestyle service. 
To combat the stigma often associated with "companion" apps, Daymate's branding heavily emphasizes boundaries and safety. 
* **The Vibe:** Fresh, friendly, and Gen-Z focused (utilizing vibrant gradients, "White Glassmorphism" UI, and interactive elements).
* **The Promise:** No awkward dates. No private locations. Just good company for the activities you love doing.

## 3. Target Audience
**Primary Audience: Urban Gen-Z & Millennials (Ages 18-35)**
* **Transplants & Expats:** Young professionals or college students who have recently moved to a new city (e.g., Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi) and haven't established a core friend group yet.
* **Introverts & Niche Hobbyists:** People who want to see a specific niche movie, try a new viral cafe, or attend a comedy show, but their current friends are busy or uninterested.
* **The "Solo-Phobic":** Individuals who experience social anxiety when attending public venues alone and are willing to pay a premium for guaranteed, vetted company.

## 4. Basic Product Flow (Frontend MVP)
The Daymate MVP was built to provide a frictionless, app-like experience directly in the browser.

* **Step 1: Landing & Trust Building**
Users land on the homepage and are immediately greeted by the "Safety First" section. Before ever entering the app, they are educated on our strict boundaries: *Verified Companions, Public Venues Only, In-App SOS Toolkit, and Strictly Platonic guidelines.*

* **Step 2: Activity Selection (No Browsing Profiles)**
To eliminate the "dating app" feel, users do not browse photos of people. Instead, they choose an activity first (Movie, Coffee, Lunch, Dinner, Comedy). 

* **Step 3: The "Vibe Check" Form**
Users input their basic details (Time, City) and use our custom "Vibe Check" interface. By selecting tags like *🗣️ Yapper* or *🛋️ Laid Back*, the matching algorithm ensures their companion matches their social energy.

* **Step 4: Monetization & Confirmation**
The user confirms their request via a simulated payment gateway (Fixed Base Fee via UPI/Card). The request is logged, and the Daymate operations team manually matches them with a verified companion via WhatsApp.
