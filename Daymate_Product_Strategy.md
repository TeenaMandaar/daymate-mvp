# Daymate – Product Strategy & MVP Overview

**What is Daymate?**
Daymate is India's first curated hangout platform for people who hate going solo. It is not a dating app. Users pick a real-world activity like coffee, a movie, or lunch, and Daymate matches them with a verified companion to join them. All hangouts are strictly platonic, at public venues only, with clear boundaries.

**Who is it for?**
Urban Gen-Z and millennials between 18-35 years old. Mainly people who are new to a city, have niche interests, or just don't want to do fun things alone.

---

## The Problem

Going solo to public activities is awkward. Your friends are busy, not interested, or cancel last minute. So you either skip the plan — or go alone and feel weird the whole time. Daymate was built to fix exactly this.

---

## How Does It Work?

1. User opens the app and picks a plan (Movie, Coffee, Lunch, Dinner, Comedy)
2. They fill in basic details — name, age, city, and preferred time
3. They complete a quick "Vibe Check" to describe their social energy
4. They pay a fixed base fee to confirm the booking
5. Daymate shows them a verified companion profile — they can **Accept** or **Skip to the next match**
6. Once accepted, Daymate confirms the hangout and reaches out via WhatsApp with final venue details

> **Note:** The companion profile is only revealed *after* the user books a plan. This keeps the platform activity-first, not profile-first — so it never feels like a dating app.

---

## Safety Features

- All companions are manually reviewed before they can be listed
- Hangouts only happen at public venues — no private locations allowed
- Users must **check in** when they arrive and **check out** when the plan ends (timeline accountability)
- An in-app SOS button is available at all times during the hangout
- Strict community guidelines ensure the experience stays strictly platonic

---

## Access & Monetization

- **Platform Fee:** A one-time curated access fee is charged at registration to filter out random, non-serious users and keep the community quality high (amount TBD by founder)
- **Per Plan Fee:** A fixed base fee is charged per booking (starting at ₹300 depending on plan)
- **Companion Cut:** Daymate takes a platform percentage from each completed booking
- Companions earn per completed plan

---

## Product Flow (MVP)

**Step 1: Landing & Trust Building**
Users land on the homepage and immediately see the "Safety First" section. Before entering the app, they learn about verified companions, public venues, the SOS toolkit, and platonic community guidelines.

**Step 2: Activity Selection (Activity-First, Not Profile-First)**
Users choose an activity first — Movie, Coffee, Lunch, Dinner, or Comedy. No browsing companion profiles upfront.

**Step 3: Vibe Check Form**
Users input their basic details (name, age, city, preferred time) and select vibe tags like *🗣️ Yapper* or *🛋️ Laid Back* so we can match their social energy.

**Step 4: Companion Profile Reveal**
After booking confirmation and payment, Daymate reveals a matched companion profile. The user can Accept or Skip to the next available match. This gives users control without making it feel like a dating app.

**Step 5: Confirmation & Timeline**
Once accepted, the user receives a confirmation with start time and end time. They check in via the app when they arrive and check out when the plan ends — ensuring accountability for both parties.
