Frequently Asked Questions

What is Daymate?

Daymate is an activity-first hangout platform. You pick a real-world plan, and we match you with a verified companion who joins you for that activity at a public venue.

Who can become a companion?

Anyone who enjoys going out and meets our strict safety and verification guidelines. Companions are selected based on trust, interests, and availability.

Is Daymate safe?

Yes. All companions go through manual verification. Plans are strictly restricted to public venues only. We do not allow private meetups, and we have clear community guidelines.

Can users directly choose companions?

No, Daymate is an activity-first platform. You choose the plan, and our system matches you with an available, verified companion who fits your preferences.

How do companions get paid?

Companions are compensated based on the duration and type of activity. Payments are securely handled through the platform.
