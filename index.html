FAQ

Got questions?
We got you.

Is Daymate a dating app?
Absolutely not. Daymate is a strictly platonic activity platform. Companions and users both agree to our community guidelines which prohibit any romantic or inappropriate behavior. Our tagline is "It's a hangout, not a date."
How are companions verified?
Every companion application is manually reviewed by our team. We check identity, run a background review process, and approve only those who meet our trust standards before they can be matched with any user.
What if I feel unsafe during a hangout?
Tap the 🚨 SOS button available directly inside the app at any time during your hangout. It instantly alerts our safety team. All plans also have a set end time so you always know when the hangout concludes.
Can I choose my companion?
Daymate is intentionally activity-first, not profile-first. You choose the plan and your vibe. Our matching team then assigns the best available, verified companion. This keeps it experience-focused, not person-focused.
How does pricing work?
Users pay a fixed base fee per plan (starting at ₹300). This covers the companion's time. Daymate takes a platform cut from each booking. Companions earn per completed plan. Simple and transparent.
